Model Architecture

Our custom PyTorch DeepLabV3+ implementation

EnhancedPreProjDeepLabV3Plus

  • Input: 12-band multispectral satellite imagery
  • Pre-projection: Conv2d → BatchNorm → ReLU → Conv2d → 3-channels
  • Backbone: ResNet101
  • Model: DeepLabV3+
  • Output: 1-channel binary segmentation mask
  • Training: Custom combo_loss, IoU metric, early stopping & LR scheduler
